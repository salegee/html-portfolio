My Top 3 Movies

In no particular order. These are simply the movies that I always think of when someone asks me what my favorite movies are.

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

Drama, Romance, Sci-Fi

A peculiar, bitter-sweet love story about how our perspective and memories define how we experience love.

Scott Pilgrim Vs The World

Romance, Comedy, Coming of Age, Comic Book

A silly, fun, coming of age teen romance that reminds me of my high school years as a dumb teenager before learning that love means huring others when you don't love yourself.

Hereditary

Psychological, Horror, Supernatural, Drama

If you've had to grow with a dysfunctional family as a result of trauma and mental health issues, this horror story will grab a hold of your anxiety from beginning to end, and remind you that the true horror comes not from literal demons, but from the ones closest to you, including yourself.
